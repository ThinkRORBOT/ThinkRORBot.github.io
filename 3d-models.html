3D models

Below, you can read about the story of some of my 3D models.

CityScape

The Cityscape is made for my IST project. It is designed to be imported into Unity. It was originally meant to be textured but I decided to not put in the effort because unity doesn't import Blender textures natively.
There are a few duplicate buildings simply because I have ran out of ideas. This is a pretty rough city as houses goes through floors, street lamps are not aligned because I ran out of time. This city was made over two weekends making smaller buildings first before moving to sky scrapers.

Detailed Jeep

This Jeep was made of a history presentation last year. It is designed to be animated and the different parts of the jeep are meant to be showcased. This presentation never happened as ideas changed. This model took two weeks to make and it is the model I speant the most amount of time on so far. It is the most detailed of my projects but there are still some niggles. The front grill has some problems and most components of the chassis are missing but development slowed down after learning the presentation was not going to happen.

Simple Speaker

This speaker took around 30 minutes to make. It was basically used to experiment with image textures. It was mainly for fun and didn't offer much challenge.

Tux

This model was made for my multimedia project. It does not really look like Tux but I tried. I don't really know why but since the project is consided to be finished. The main problem was the feet where I can't get the toes to be more rounded and the proportions not being correct.

Blender Logo

This is the blender logo I tried to make. It really didn't have much of a purpose. The fins that stretch out from the logo were the main challenge points. The fins aren't wide enough and I stuggled to get them to be even with each other and for them to be the same angle.

Space Ships

Link 1: https://grabcad.com/library/space-ship-4
Link 2: https://grabcad.com/library/space-ship-3
These ships were made for a multimedia project last year. It was designed to be used in a trailer for a movie. These models are not detailed and are not perfect but I was pretty pleased with them at that time. It might be of some use to someone later though.

CO2 Car

This was a prototype CO2 car for the competition. It was not used because it broke every rule possible. Still, it was a good concept-the front splitter was designed to direct the air into the wheels which are shaped like a fan to keep the speed. There are many things missing from this model including wheels, axle, and a canister holder.

Basic Car

This is a basic car made following a tutorial from youtube. It does not look that good but I leant proportional editing as well as loopcuts in that tutorial. It can still serve as a template for the other cars I might make.
