This is the first series of photographs I have taken with a proper camera(Sony a6000). The first photographs taken were a learning journey, infact the whole journey was a learning journey, trying different modes, different composition of subjects, etc. Hopefully, photographs are going to be better in the future with less repetitiveness. There won't be many more large photoshoots like this unless it's another multi-month trip. Other albums are likely to be much smaller.
