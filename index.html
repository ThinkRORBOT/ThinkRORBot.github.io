About Me

I am, for now, just a student and hobbiest interested in technology and electronics.

I have been a Blender user for over a year doing tutorials, making my own models, using it for assessments etc. I have programmed for less than a year, using the majority of that time reading books but I have made a few programs/scripts in c++ and looking to eventually contribute to Opensource. I have been a photographer for less than a year but I rarely use my camera outside of special occations or holidays. Other programs I use on and off are GIMP, Inkscape, Kdenlinve. I have been a full time Linux user for over a year.

Three Industries of work in one place.
